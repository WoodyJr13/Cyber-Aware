Cybersecurity Resources

Spam Call & Text Blockers

These apps help block unwanted spam calls and filter suspicious text messages, reducing your exposure to scams.

  • Nomorobo: Excellent for automatically blocking robocalls.
  • Truecaller: Helps identify unknown callers and block spam.
  • Hiya: Provides caller ID and spam protection.

Password Managers

Password managers create, store, and fill in strong, unique passwords for all your accounts, which is one of the most effective security habits.

  • Bitwarden: A popular open-source option with a robust free version.
  • 1Password: Known for its user-friendly design and family sharing features.
  • LastPass: A widely-used manager that offers a solid set of features.

Antivirus & Anti-Malware Software

Reliable antivirus software is essential for protecting your devices from malware, ransomware, and other threats.

  • Malwarebytes: A top-rated tool for finding and removing malware.
  • Bitdefender Antivirus: Offers excellent protection with minimal impact on system performance.
  • Windows Defender: Built into Windows, it provides solid, free, and convenient protection.
