About the Creator

CyberAware was created by Ved Shah, a rising sophomore at David Brearley High School in New Jersey. As the captain of his school's robotics team and an avid enthusiast of all things tech and STEM, Ved has always been passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems.

After participating in the NJCCIC Cybersecurity Basics program, Ved was inspired to take action. He recognized that in a world where technology is everywhere, vulnerable groups of people often face the greatest risks online. This realization motivated him to build a resource that could help bridge that gap.

This website is the result of that vision. It was designed to make cybersecurity education accessible, engaging, and practical for everyone, helping users build the essential skills needed to stay safe in an increasingly digital world.
